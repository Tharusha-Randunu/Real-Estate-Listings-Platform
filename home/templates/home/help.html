Posting Property Ads

How do I post my property on LandMark?

To post a property, you first need to create an account or log in. Once you're logged in, click on "Post an Ad" at the top navigation bar. You'll be guided through the process:

  • Select whether you're selling, renting, or leasing.
  • Provide detailed property information (location, type, price, size, etc.).
  • Upload high-quality images that best represent your property.
  • Optionally add a map location, property features, and a compelling description.
  • Review your ad and click "Submit". Your listing will be reviewed before being published.

Once approved, your property will be visible to thousands of interested buyers or renters.

How do I add images to my ad?

After filling in property details, the image upload section will appear. You can:

  • Click "Add Images" to browse and upload from your device.
  • Drag and drop multiple files directly into the upload area.
  • Ensure each image is clear, well-lit, and represents key areas of the property (exterior, bedrooms, kitchen, etc.).
You can reorder images by dragging them after upload.

How do I find geo data for my ad?

Use our "Get Location" tool in the form, or alternatively visit this geo location tool to retrieve precise latitude and longitude for your property. Input those values into the relevant fields before submitting your ad.

Can I sell or rent through LandMark directly?

We are a listing platform and do not directly sell or rent properties. However, we connect you to a vast audience and offer tools to track interest. If you require professional agent services, we can recommend trusted partners through our Find an Agent service.

Managing Your Ads

How do I edit or delete my property listing?

Login to your dashboard. Under the "My Posted Ads" section:

  • Click the 'Edit Ad' button to edit the ad details, images, or price.
  • To delete an ad, click the 'Delete Ad' button. A confirmation prompt will appear to avoid accidental deletion.

How do I find my Ad?

Login to your dashboard. Under the "My Posted Ads" section you will find all the Ads you have posted.

Buying Property in Sri Lanka

Where can I get market insights?

Visit our Market Insights page, which includes:

  • Market Value Estimator

Can I get help from a professional agent?

Yes! If you're new to property investment or relocation, we recommend working with a trusted agent. Visit our Find an Agent page to browse licensed professionals, filtered by location, property type, and experience.

Profile Settings

How do I update my profile photo?

Go to your Dashboard > Edit Profile. You can:

  • Click on change profile picture.
  • Upload a new image (JPG or PNG, up to 5MB).
  • Save changes.
